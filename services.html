About Us:

Digital Lync Technologies founded by Manikanta Kona in 2016 is headquartered in San Francisco Bay Area, CA with centers in Hyderabad, Telangana. Digital Lync Education Hub is a platform for enthusiastic learners to learn, experiment, innovate and contribute to future technologies and make great careers. Our strength lies in R&D. At Digital Lync Technologies, we believe in making the learning place a workplace and the workplace a learning place.
